## Academic & Peer-Reviewed Sources

### Chemistry & Sensory Analysis

1. **Chen Y, et al. (2023)**
   - Title: "Estimating the sensory-associated metabolites profiling of matcha based on PDO attributes"
   - Journal: *Heliyon* 9(11)
   - PMC ID: PMC10654251
   - URL: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10654251/
   - Key Content: L-theanine to catechin ratios, sensory quality correlations

2. **Wei W, et al. (2025)**
   - Title: "Construction and Application of a Sensory Flavor Wheel for Matcha"
   - Journal: *China Tea Processing*
   - Key Content: Standardized flavor wheel terminology, sensory descriptors

3. **Wu J, et al. (2022)**
   - Title: "Physicochemical indicators for matcha sensory quality"
   - Journal: *Food Chemistry* 371
   - Key Content: Chemical markers predicting taste quality

4. **Zhang et al. (2018)**
   - Title: "Quantitative analyses of bitterness and astringency of catechins"
   - Journal: *Food Chemistry*
   - Key Content: Catechin taste thresholds, bitterness mechanisms

5. **Kochman et al. (2020)**
   - Title: "Health Benefits and Chemical Composition of Matcha Green Tea"
   - Journal: *Molecules*
   - PMC ID: PMC7796401
   - URL: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC7796401/
   - Key Content: Comprehensive chemical composition analysis

6. **Frontiers in Plant Science (2017)**
   - Title: "L-Theanine Content and Related Gene Expression"
   - Key Content: Shading effects on amino acid accumulation

7. **PMC (2023)**
   - Title: "Catechin Composition, Phenolic Content of Matcha Green Teas"
   - Key Content: Comparative analysis across grades

## Citation Format for Training Data

When creating training data responses, the chatbot should NOT cite sources directly to customers. However, the knowledge base ensures all information is traceable and verifiable.

For internal documentation, use format:
```
[Source #X] Claim text
```

Example:
```
[Source #10] Nishio produces approximately 60% of Japan's matcha market share.
[Source #1] Premium matcha contains 16-21+ mg/g of L-theanine.
```
